# Treatment

## Title

05/12/2026: Lucid King Conduit

## Logline

After sleep paralysis drops Jet onto a starlit beach with a strangely ordinary toucan, he follows a dream shift into a city of superheroes where his missing Lucid Light has become a tyrant king who can only be changed through Jet's hidden connection to his core.

## Central Conflict

Jet cannot command Lucid Light directly. The power has separated from him and become the city's ruler, while frightened superhumans prepare to overthrow the king by force. Jet must decide whether to join their rebellion or trust the subtler conduit bond that still links him to the Lucid Light inside the king.

## Theme

Lucid power is not only control over reality; it can also be influence, empathy, and recognition when power takes a life of its own.

## Ending

Jet reaches through the Lucid Light at the king's core and changes his heart. The tyrant lowers his crown, opens the city gates, and becomes a king of the people while Jet realizes that conduit lucidity may be becoming his new normal.

## Emotional Arc

Jet moves from confusion, to wonder, to restraint, to focused compassion, to uncertain acceptance as he learns that his missing power is not gone but living outside his body.

## Page Count

Eight pages: cover plus seven story pages.
