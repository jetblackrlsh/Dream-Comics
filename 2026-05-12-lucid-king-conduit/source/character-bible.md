# Character Bible

## Jet

Adult man with dark-brown skin, full dark-brown beard, warm brown eyes, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a black hoodie over a "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. Signature items are a colorful futuristic crystal rainbow sword and a rope-like golden glittering energy tether. In this story, his usual sparkling purple Lucid Light is absent from his hands, so his posture often reads cautious, curious, and focused instead of omnipotent.

## The Toucan

An ordinary tropical toucan with a glossy black body, white throat, bright orange-yellow beak, small alert eyes, and no mystical marks, clothing, powers, or magical props. It is intentionally normal and should look slightly out of place in the cosmic lucid setting.

## The Lucid King

A superhuman ruler formed from Jet's missing Lucid Light. He has short black buzzcut hair, sharp regal features, glowing purple eyes, a regal purple cheetah-print cape, and a fitted gold-and-red futuristic leather bodysuit with metallic seams. His chest contains a visible starry purple Lucid Light core. Early pages show him stern, elevated, and tyrannical; later pages show softened eyes, lowered shoulders, and a protective public-spirited presence. He is not redesigned between pages.

## Rebel Superhumans

A small crowd of colorful superpowered city defenders who oppose the Lucid King. They wear varied heroic costumes and move through the air with flight trails, elemental powers, and energy effects. Keep them secondary to Jet and the Lucid King. They should look brave and nervous rather than villainous.

## Setting Motifs

The dream begins on a starlit beach with palm trees and a dark ocean. The city beyond the palms is a bright superhero metropolis with glowing towers, sky bridges, aerial traffic, and crowds of caped figures. Lucid Light appears as sparkling purple energy like a night sky full of stars, concentrated in the Lucid King's chest core rather than Jet's hands.
