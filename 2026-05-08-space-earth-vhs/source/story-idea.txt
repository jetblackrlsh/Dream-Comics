Dream journal entry dated 05/08/2026.

I induced lucidity via sleep paralysis.

This dream takes place in the same continuity of lucid dreams that started on 06/11/2025 and has continued to the present. These dreams take place in the Storyverse. The Storyverse is a multiverse where all possible story ideas exist as cosmic bubble universes that float together in a cosmic void.

My name is Jet. General description: Man with dark-brown skin, full dark-brown beard, long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie over a "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, black cross-body belt bag, warm brown eyes. I have a rainbow sword, resembling a colorful futuristic crystal sword, and an energy tether, resembling a rope made of golden glittery light.

I am immediately greeted by an incredibly bright light that for a moment makes me think that I am in the waking world simply due to how real it seems.

It was the light of the sun shining down on the Earth from space.

I am alone floating in space and looking at the Earth.

I look at the Earth from multiple angles and perspectives as I teleport around the planet.

No matter where I teleport out in space, the Earth looks absolutely gorgeous as the sun shines on one side and shadows cover the other.

Before bed, I read a lot of hype about UFO files potentially being released the next morning.

I thought about that while looking down at the Earth, but I didn't see any UFOs.

I saw glimpses of aliens just peacefully floating in space.

I saw the Earth shatter and reform.

It was like watching a VHS tape play and rewind itself.

The entire experience was mystical, surreal, and hyperrealistic.

It truly felt like I was in space.
