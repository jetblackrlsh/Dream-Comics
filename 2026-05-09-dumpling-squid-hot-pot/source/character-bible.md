# Character Bible

## Jet

Man with dark-brown skin, full dark-brown beard, warm brown eyes, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a black hoodie over a "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. Signature props: a colorful futuristic crystal rainbow sword and a rope-like energy tether made of golden glittery light. His expressions move from startled panic to suspicious focus to relaxed amusement.

## Storyverse Kitchen Chefs

Three recurring chefs with friendly but uncanny designs. They wear tall white chef hats, white coats with bright teal trim, and heavy heatproof gloves. Their faces are expressive and kind but slightly dreamlike: glowing eyes, exaggerated smiles, and colorful anime silhouettes. They should not look monstrous or predatory. Their body language is welcoming, confused by Jet's fear, and proud of the food.

## Dumpling Squids

Small living food creatures shaped like steamed dumplings with tiny squid tentacles, shiny black dot eyes, and soft folded dough bodies. They bob in the broth, swim gently, and look whimsical rather than disgusting. Some appear whole and playful; one is shown as a bite-sized dream-food morsel without gore.

## Cauldron Kitchen

A vast Storyverse kitchen floating inside a cosmic void with faint bubble universes visible beyond arched windows. The main prop is an enormous round metal hot pot filled with golden broth, steam, and dumpling squids. The scene should feel hot, bright, surreal, and oddly cozy.
