# Treatment

## Logline

After inducing lucidity through sleep paralysis, Jet awakens in orbit above Earth, expecting signs of hidden UFO disclosure but instead receiving a serene cosmic vision of Earth breaking and rewinding like a VHS tape.

## Central Conflict

Jet searches the hyperreal scene for proof of the next day's promised UFO files, but the dream refuses to become a spectacle. The conflict is internal: whether to chase evidence or accept the vast, mystical reality unfolding in front of him.

## Theme

Some visions are not evidence to collect. They are experiences to witness.

## Ending

Jet watches Earth shatter and reform, understanding that the dream is showing him the living story of the planet rather than a secret hidden above it.

## Emotional Arc

Disorientation -> awe -> curiosity -> expectation -> surprise -> surrender -> reverence.

## Cast

- Jet: the dreamer and lucid traveler, alone in space for this entry.
- Peaceful aliens: brief glimpses of unknown beings floating calmly in orbit, not antagonists.
- Earth: treated as the central visual presence, beautiful, fragile, and impossible.
