# Character Bible

## Jet as the Anamorphis UAP

Jet spends this story transformed into a luminous blue, faceless, flying, shape-shifting humanoid. He has no visible human face, hair, clothing, sword, tether, or ordinary body details while transformed. His body is smooth bright blue living light with a subtle humanoid silhouette, long flexible limbs, and a glowing aura. He can dissolve into particles, open holes through his torso, stretch his arms great distances, and release radiant bursts of light. Keep him readable as the same luminous blue faceless being on every page.

## Green Hand

Superhero member of the Three Hands. He has spiky brown hair, athletic build, and a sleek green power suit with armored panels, glowing green gauntlets, and green energy emitters in the palms. His expression is bold and hotheaded. His powers appear as bright green energy blasts and flight trails. After being defeated, he is unconscious but still visibly Green Hand.

## Red Hand

Superhero member of the Three Hands. He has spiky black hair, athletic build, and a red combat battle suit with angular armor plates. His hands can transform into roaring chainsaw blades, with metal teeth and red-orange sparks. His expression is aggressive and fast-moving. After being defeated, he is unconscious but still visibly Red Hand.

## Black Hand

The most dangerous member of the Three Hands. He is bald, stern, and wears a black combat suit with matte armor, dark metallic gloves, and subtle gravity-ring details. His power creates circular black holes with warped star fields, bent light, and dark gravitational halos. He should look composed and heavy with threat until the final collision knocks him unconscious.

## Setting Motifs

The opening location is a moonlit tropical beach under a star-filled sky, continuing from the prior dream. The fight quickly climbs into the open night sky above the beach, with ocean reflections below and occasional hints of a superhero world on the horizon. The Anamorphis form glows blue-white, Green Hand's power glows green, Red Hand's chainsaws throw red-orange sparks, and Black Hand's black holes bend the starlight.
