# Character Bible

## Jet

Jet is a lucid dream traveler with dark-brown skin, warm brown eyes, a full dark-brown beard, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a "Dream Anime" graphic tee under the hoodie, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. He carries a rainbow sword that resembles a colorful futuristic crystal sword and an energy tether that resembles a rope made of golden glittery light. In this comic Jet mostly appears during the sleep-paralysis opening and final reflective imagery. Keep his dreadlocks, beard, hoodie, tee, pants stripe, white sneakers, and belt bag consistent.

## Black Out

Black Out is Jet transformed into a different heroic identity. He is a faceless, solid-black humanoid entity with a sleek athletic superhero silhouette, no visible eyes or mouth, and skin that looks like living shadow. Electric energy radiates from his fists, forearms, shoulders, chest, and nervous system like branching white-blue lightning under a black surface. His outline glows with electric cyan, violet, and stark white highlights. He moves with augmented speed, leaving jagged lightning afterimages. He should always remain faceless, solid black, powerful, and slightly alien, with electricity visibly integrated into his body rather than worn as equipment.

## The Woman At The Party

The targeted woman is a stylish adult party guest with medium-brown skin, expressive dark eyes, and voluminous shoulder-length auburn curls. She wears an elegant teal party dress with silver geometric jewelry and black heels. She is warm, curious, and briefly drawn toward Black Out before danger finds her. Keep the teal dress, auburn curls, silver jewelry, and startled but resilient expression consistent.

## The Explosives Rival

The explosives rival is a dangerous adult villain and jealous rival of Black Out. He has pale skin, sharp cheekbones, short platinum hair, narrow amber eyes, and a scorched crimson bomber jacket over black tactical clothes. He carries compact glowing orange explosive devices and detonator gauntlets. His visual motif is orange blast light, cracked warning symbols, smoke, and jagged metal fragments. Keep the crimson jacket, platinum hair, amber eyes, and detonator gauntlets consistent. Depict his defeat in stylized non-gory comic action, with cracked armor, smoke, bruised silhouette, and disorientation rather than graphic injury.

## Black Out's Followers

Black Out's followers are a small school of electric-power students and celebrity admirers. They wear sleek black training jackets with cyan lightning trim, fingerless gloves, and nervous excited expressions. They gather around him at the surprise party with admiration, notebooks, phone cameras, and sparks of beginner electricity. They should read as people who want to learn how to become as powerful as Black Out.

## Surprise Party Venue

The party takes place in a high-rise penthouse training hall converted into a celebration space. It has glossy black floors, cyan neon lightning decor, floating holographic banners, reflective windows showing a futuristic skyline, tables with bright party lights, and many unfamiliar guests. The room should feel glamorous but unstable, like a celebrity event inside a superhero headquarters.

## Dark Void And Electricity

The opening void is almost pure darkness, but it behaves like living fabric wrapping Jet's body. Electricity should pierce the black space with white-blue branching light. The void becomes Black Out's skin, so the transformation should visually connect sleep paralysis, darkness, nervous-system stimulation, and electric power.
