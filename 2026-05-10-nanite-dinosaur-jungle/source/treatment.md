# Treatment

## Title

05/10/2026: Nanite Dinosaur Jungle

## Logline

After entering a Storyverse jungle through sleep paralysis, Jet is transformed into a nanite warrior and must defeat a territorial humanoid T-Rex before a flying guardian accepts him as a genuine superpowered ally.

## Central Conflict

Jet expects Lucid Light to answer him, but this world gives him a stranger weapon: a body made of living metal nanites. The conflict is both external, surviving the dinosaur's attack, and internal, trusting a power he did not choose.

## Theme

Lucid discovery means adapting when the dream changes the rules.

## Ending

Jet reforms as himself after the battle. A caped woman who doubted him rewards his victory by carrying him high above the jungle, turning the violent encounter into a triumphant aerial rush.

## Emotional Arc

Jet moves from disorientation, to alarm, to fascinated experimentation, to confident combat, and finally to exhilarated wonder.

## Page Count

Eight pages: cover plus seven story pages.
