This dream journal entry is dated 05/13/2026.

Jet induced lucidity through sleep paralysis and returned to the beach from the previous night's dream. The dream belongs to the ongoing Storyverse continuity that began on 06/11/2025, where possible story ideas exist as cosmic bubble universes floating in a cosmic void.

On the beach, Jet takes the form of an Anamorphis UAP: a luminous blue, faceless, flying, shape-shifting humanoid. He launches into the sky, excited to test the form after watching a video of an Anamorphis UAP before bed. The form feels powerful, alien, and visually unlike anything around him.

The Three Hands, a superhero team from that world, mistake Jet's otherworldly appearance for a monster. Jet understands why they think that. Their world is full of superhumans who know each other, so a faceless outsider with immense power seems dangerous. He does not correct them because he wants a fight to test his abilities.

Green Hand wears a green power suit, has spiky brown hair, and fires green energy blasts. Jet splits his luminous body into particles to dodge the blasts. When Green Hand flies in to punch him, Jet opens a hole through his stomach so the punch passes through harmlessly. Jet closes the hole around Green Hand's arm and blasts him point blank with light, sending him crashing down.

Red Hand has spiky black hair, wears a red combat battle suit, and can transform his hands into chainsaws. Red Hand attacks at high speed, but Jet's luminous form cannot simply be cut apart. Jet radiates a huge flash of light that stuns and blinds Red Hand, then slams both fists down on his head, knocking him unconscious out of the sky.

Black Hand is bald, wears a black combat suit, and can create black holes. He is far more dangerous than the others because Jet's light body is vulnerable to the pull of black holes. Jet cannot outrun them, so he stretches his arm to the ground, picks up Green Hand and Red Hand, and throws them toward Black Hand's black holes. Black Hand closes the black holes to save his allies. The unconscious heroes crash into him, knocking him unconscious.

Impressed by the Anamorphis form's abilities, Jet flies off into the starlit sky.
