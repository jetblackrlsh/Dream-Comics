# Treatment

## Logline

After inducing lucidity through sleep paralysis, Jet materializes inside a boiling cauldron full of living dumpling squids, mistakes a surreal feast for an attempt to cook him, then relaxes when the chefs reveal that the pot is simply where the leftover food has been waiting.

## Central Conflict

Jet must decide whether the chefs are threatening him or offering impossible hospitality. The conflict is comic and internal: panic turns into suspicion, suspicion turns into taste, and taste turns into an uneasy but enjoyable acceptance.

## Theme

A dream can feel dangerous before it reveals that its logic is stranger than its threat.

## Ending

Jet settles into the pot like a hot tub, eating dumpling squids while still wondering why the chefs arranged his arrival inside the food instead of next to it.

## Emotional Arc

Sleep paralysis doorway -> sudden panic -> defensive confusion -> hesitant trust -> delighted surprise -> logical doubt -> relaxed enjoyment.

## Cast

- Jet: the lucid dreamer, alone for this entry, carrying his rainbow crystal sword and golden energy tether.
- The Chefs: cheerful Storyverse kitchen beings who seem suspicious at first but insist they are sharing leftovers.
- Dumpling Squids: living dumplings shaped like tiny squids, cute, strange, and edible in dream logic.
- The Cauldron Kitchen: a surreal cosmic restaurant-kitchen floating in the Storyverse, warm and absurd instead of sinister.
