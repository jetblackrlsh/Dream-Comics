# Character Bible

## Jet

Adult man with dark-brown skin, full dark-brown beard, warm brown eyes, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a black hoodie over a "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. His signature items are a colorful futuristic rainbow crystal sword and a rope-like golden glittering energy tether. His posture is alert and adaptive, shifting from startled to intensely focused to delighted.

## Nanite Form

Jet's temporary alternate body appears as a tan-skinned adult man with spiky brown hair, orange goggles, a red jacket, black pants, and black boots. The body can split apart into glittering silver-gray metal nanites. The hands can merge into a giant reflective metal shield. In full cloud state, the body becomes a dense mist of tiny chrome particles with red jacket fragments implied in the silhouette.

Continuity note: this is still Jet experiencing the form. The comic should make the transformation clear but avoid presenting the nanite form as a separate person.

## Humanoid T-Rex

Large brown humanoid T-Rex with a powerful dinosaur head, heavy tail, muscular torso, clawed hands, thick legs, amber eyes, and a territorial fighter's stance. It has no weapons or armor. Its expressions range from charging fury to confused frustration to dazed unconsciousness. Keep the action non-graphic.

## Flying Woman

Adult woman with black hair styled into two space buns, a white fitted bodysuit with red accents, a red cape, and white boots. She descends from the sky with controlled superhero confidence. Her expressions shift from skeptical and guarded to impressed and playful.

## Setting

A lush alien jungle inside the Storyverse. The sky occasionally reveals distant cosmic bubble universes beyond the canopy. Purple Lucid Light appears as sparkling night-sky energy at the moment of arrival, but the combat power in this issue is silver nanite metal rather than purple magic.
