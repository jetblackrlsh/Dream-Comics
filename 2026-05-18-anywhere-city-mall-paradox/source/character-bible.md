# Character Bible

## Jet

Jet is a lucid dream traveler with dark-brown skin, warm brown eyes, a full dark-brown beard, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a black hoodie over a "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. He carries a rainbow sword that resembles a colorful futuristic crystal sword and an energy tether that resembles a rope made of golden glittery light. In this issue he also holds a sleek black card with faint rainbow and purple Lucid Light reflections. Keep his dreadlocks, beard, hoodie, tee, pants stripe, white sneakers, belt bag, black card, rainbow sword, and golden energy tether consistent.

## Anywhere City

Anywhere City is a dream city that contains at least one of every building anyone can imagine. The issue only needs glimpses of it: a bank, police station, hospital, apartment building, library, theater, arcade, observatory, and other impossible structures arranged together on a bright surreal skyline. The city should feel orderly but absurdly complete.

## Anywhere City Mall

Anywhere City Mall is the city's single mall and contains every item currently for sale. Its interior should feel impossibly large: glass atriums, luminous escalators, stacked balconies, catalog-like departments, floating directories, and endless shelves of products. Avoid readable brand names. Show objects from every category: clothing, electronics, art supplies, food, vehicles, books, toys, tools, furniture, appliances, instruments, rare collectibles, and strange Storyverse artifacts.

## The Black Card

The black card provides unlimited funds. It is glossy, minimal, and mysterious, with no readable numbers or real-world branding. It reflects purple Lucid Light like a night sky full of stars. When Jet considers buying the entire mall, the card becomes visually powerful and ominous.

## Lucid Light

Lucid Light is sparkling purple reality-warping energy that shines like a night sky full of stars. It appears in the sleep-paralysis opening, around Jet's awareness, in the mall's impossible inventory magic, and in the final paradox loop. It should not replace Jet's golden energy tether or rainbow sword; it is a cosmic ambient force.

## City Shoppers

The shoppers are ordinary dream citizens of many ages, body types, and futuristic-streetwear styles. They should appear as people who depend on the mall as the city's only source of goods. Keep them sympathetic rather than faceless: families checking price tags, workers buying tools, artists choosing supplies, and travelers browsing gear.

## The Paradox

The paradox is the visual antagonist. It has no body. It appears as recursive shelves, impossible receipts, an infinity-shaped shopping cart path, items turning into purple starlight when marked "not for sale," and a glowing loop between the black card, the mall deed, empty shelves, and restored abundance.
