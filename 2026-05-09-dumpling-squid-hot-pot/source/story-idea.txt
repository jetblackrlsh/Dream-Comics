Dream journal entry dated 05/09/2026.

I induced lucidity via sleep paralysis.

This dream takes place in the same continuity of lucid dreams that started on 06/11/2025 and has continued to the present. These dreams take place in the Storyverse, a multiverse where all possible story ideas exist as cosmic bubble universes floating together in a cosmic void.

My name is Jet. I am a man with dark-brown skin, a full dark-brown beard, long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie over a "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, a black cross-body belt bag, and warm brown eyes. I have a rainbow sword that resembles a colorful futuristic crystal sword and an energy tether that resembles a rope made of golden glittery light.

Alone, I appear in a pot of living squids made of dumplings.

I freak out at first, but the chefs assure me that they are just giving me their leftover food.

I take a bite out of one of the dumpling squids, and I am surprised by how good they taste.

I initially assumed that I was inside the pot because they were trying to cook me.

The chefs question why they would even try to eat me since they clearly have plenty of dumpling squids to eat.

They were right that they clearly had plenty of food and would not need to resort to trying to eat people.

One thing I did not really ask them about is why they set it up where I would arrive inside the pot of boiling water. If they just wanted to share their food, they could have set the pot next to where I arrived instead of right where I arrived.

But regardless of whatever their true intentions were, in the end it felt like eating in a hot tub rather than being cooked alive.

This dream was weird, but overall a fun and enjoyable time.
