Dream journal date: 05/11/2026.

Jet induces lucidity through sleep paralysis and arrives alone on a Storyverse world where he is the manager of super powered celebrities. In this world he decides which celebrities rise to the top and which flop, making high-stakes career decisions that shape futures.

The strangest part of the dream is a transit choice. Angel-driven buses arrive, each designed to carry beings of incredible power. Every bus option seems too dangerous, and Jet's normal Lucid Light abilities are not fully available, so he skips the buses entirely.

Jet still makes it home through a strange comic book that appears to feed on his Lucid Light. The book seems alive, acting as a conduit for Jet's awareness. Instead of directly controlling reality, Jet experiences control as if split into two entities: the conscious lucid control is expressed through an inanimate comic book, while his embodied dream-self must use the book to harness that power.

Core adaptation angle: a lucid dream about delegated control, where Jet survives by trusting a living comic book as the conduit for his awareness.
