# Treatment

## Title

05/17/2026: Black Out Electric Lucidity

## Logline

After inducing lucidity through sleep paralysis, Jet is swallowed by a dark void and transformed into Black Out, a faceless electric celebrity hero whose power lets him save a targeted woman from an explosion, but whose altered instincts reveal the danger of becoming someone else through amplified nerves and electricity.

## Central Conflict

Jet is aware inside Black Out's body but does not fully control the identity, memories, or emotional momentum that come with it. He must protect an innocent woman from a jealous explosives specialist while realizing that Black Out's electric power is also changing how he thinks, reacts, and punishes.

## Theme

Power that enhances the body can also rewrite the self; lucidity is not only control over a dream, but awareness of who is doing the controlling.

## Ending

Black Out saves the woman and defeats the bomber, but the victory ends in a charged mirror of the dark void. Jet recognizes Black Out as a possible version of himself shaped by nervous-system stimulation and chooses to remember the boundary between ability and identity.

## Emotional Arc

Jet begins isolated in sleep-paralysis darkness, then feels awe as the void becomes a second skin and transforms him into Black Out. At the party, he is disoriented by strangers who know the body he is wearing better than he does. The explosion snaps him into action and anger. After the violent rescue, his lucidity sharpens into reflection: he was aware and acting, but the electric identity was steering him too.

## Page Count

Eight pages: cover plus seven story pages.
