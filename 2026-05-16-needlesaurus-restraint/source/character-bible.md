# Character Bible

## Jet

Jet is a lucid dream traveler with dark-brown skin, warm brown eyes, a full dark-brown beard, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a "Dream Anime" graphic tee under the hoodie, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. He carries a rainbow sword that resembles a colorful futuristic crystal sword and an energy tether that resembles a rope made of golden glittery light. In this comic he manifests a pair of golden wristbands of power that shine with sparkling gold energy, create translucent golden force fields, and propel him on streams of golden energy. Keep his dreadlocks, beard, hoodie, tee, pants stripe, white sneakers, belt bag, rainbow sword, and golden wristbands consistent on every page.

## Needlesaurus

Needlesaurus is a massive humanoid dinosaur built from millions of interlocked metal needles. It has a tyrannosaur-like head, hunched humanoid shoulders, long clawed arms made from needle bundles, thick digitigrade legs, a heavy spiked tail, porcupine quill ridges, red sensor eyes, and constantly shifting chrome plates made of needle rods. It towers over city streets and attacks with spear-like needle storms, sweeping claws, and crushing stomps. It should feel frightening but also exhausted by the middle of the story.

## Freed Needleman

The freed Needleman is the person trapped inside Needlesaurus. He is a humanoid figure made of interlocking silver metal needles with red sensor eyes, blade-like fingers, and a thin chrome silhouette. Unlike the army of Needlemen, he looks frightened, tired, and ashamed after being freed. By the final page he wears golden wristbands of restraint on both wrists, glowing softly to keep his unstable power contained.

## Needlemen

Needlemen are humanoid monsters made of metal needles. Their bodies are modular clusters of interlocking silver needle rods, jointed needle spines, blade-like fingers, and porcupine-like projectile quills. They have red sensor slits and cold chrome reflections. In this comic most of them appear as broken background threats left from the invasion, showing that Needlesaurus was the central force behind the attack.

## Storyverse City

The battle takes place in the same dense futuristic Storyverse city from the previous Needlemen dream. Use shattered streets, glass towers, elevated transit rails, emergency lights, smoke columns, abandoned cars, and bright teal city signage. The city should stay readable and colorful despite the destruction, with gold force fields, chrome needle reflections, red alarms, and faint purple Storyverse sky-glow.

## Golden Wristbands And Restraint

Jet's golden wristbands of power appear as polished gold bands around both wrists, engraved with luminous geometric dream symbols. They generate translucent honey-gold hexagonal force fields and comet-like streams of golden energy. The restraint wristbands given to the freed Needleman use the same visual language but glow more softly, symbolizing control rather than attack.
