# Treatment

## Title

05/15/2026: Needle Men Power Band

## Logline

After inducing lucidity through sleep paralysis, Jet lands in a besieged Storyverse city where a stranger in a purple battle suit recognizes him, questions his missing golden power band, and forces him to prove that he can defend his world without it.

## Central Conflict

Jet wants to act without waiting for permission or inherited power, while the purple-suited stranger treats the golden power band as the expected symbol of responsibility. The Needle Men invasion turns their argument into a test: if this city is truly Jet's world to defend, he must protect it using his own lucid tools.

## Theme

Responsibility is proven through action, not through possessing the expected badge of authority.

## Ending

Jet's fearless battle inspires the purple-suited stranger to join him. The fight remains unfinished when Jet wakes, leaving the deeper mystery unresolved: how did this stranger know Jet, and why did he call this endangered world Jet's responsibility?

## Emotional Arc

Jet begins startled and curious when he is recognized by someone he does not know. He becomes defiant when the stranger judges him for lacking a golden power band, then confident when he chooses to fight anyway. His pain and perseverance turn into momentum, and the stranger's respect begins to shift before the dream ends.

## Page Count

Eight pages: cover plus seven story pages.
