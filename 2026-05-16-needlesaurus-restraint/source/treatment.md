# Treatment

## Title

05/16/2026: Needlesaurus Restraint

## Logline

After inducing lucidity through sleep paralysis, Jet returns alone to the Needlemen-besieged Storyverse city and faces their towering leader, only to discover that the monstrous Needlesaurus is really an overwhelmed Needleman trapped inside his own uncontrolled power.

## Central Conflict

Jet wants to defeat the leader of the Needlemen invasion before the city falls, while the Needlesaurus overwhelms him with attacks that even golden force fields cannot block forever. The deeper conflict appears when Jet realizes the monster is a person in crisis, and that victory requires restraint and rescue instead of destruction.

## Theme

Not everything that looks monstrous is evil; sometimes people need help learning how to control their power.

## Ending

Jet breaks through the Needlesaurus, frees the Needleman trapped in its core, and gives him golden wristbands of restraint so he can live peacefully in the city without transforming again.

## Emotional Arc

Jet begins ready for a final battle with the apparent leader of the invasion. His confidence becomes strain as the force fields crack under relentless needle attacks. When he sees the trapped core, his goal shifts from defeating a monster to saving a person. He ends with compassion and cautious hope instead of triumphal anger.

## Page Count

Eight pages: cover plus seven story pages.
