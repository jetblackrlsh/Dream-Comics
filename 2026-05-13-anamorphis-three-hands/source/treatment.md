# Treatment

## Title

05/13/2026: Anamorphis Three Hands

## Logline

After sleep paralysis returns Jet to a familiar dream beach, he transforms into a luminous blue Anamorphis UAP and tests its strange powers against the Three Hands, a superhero team that mistakes his faceless alien form for a monster.

## Central Conflict

Jet wants to understand the limits of his new shape-shifting light body, but the Three Hands interpret his arrival as a threat. Each hero forces Jet to solve a different combat problem: energy blasts, physical blades, and finally black holes that can consume his light.

## Theme

Power changes how others read you. In a world that mistakes the unfamiliar for monstrous, Jet chooses curiosity and restraint, using the fight to learn rather than to destroy.

## Ending

Jet defeats Black Hand by forcing him to save Green Hand and Red Hand from his own black holes. With the Three Hands unconscious but alive below, Jet flies away into the starlit sky, impressed by the Anamorphis form and ready to keep exploring.

## Emotional Arc

Jet begins excited and experimental, becomes amused by the mistaken monster fight, shifts into tactical focus when Black Hand's black holes threaten him, and ends exhilarated by the discovery that his alien form can win through improvisation.

## Page Count

Eight pages: cover plus seven story pages.
