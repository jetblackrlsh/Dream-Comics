# Character Bible

## Jet

Jet is a lucid dream traveler with dark-brown skin, warm brown eyes, a full dark-brown beard, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a "Dream Anime" graphic tee under the hoodie, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. He carries a rainbow sword that resembles a colorful futuristic crystal sword and an energy tether that resembles a rope made of golden glittery light. In this comic he also manifests Lucid Light shears made of sparkling purple, starry energy. Keep his dreadlocks, beard, hoodie, tee, pants stripe, white sneakers, belt bag, rainbow sword, and purple Lucid Light consistent on every page. His expression range moves from startled curiosity to confident defiance to battle-focused resolve.

## Purple Suit

Purple Suit is a mysterious stranger who already knows Jet. He is a helmetless adult man in a sleek futuristic purple armored battle suit with dark violet panels, black undersuit seams, and a bright golden power band locked around his right forearm. The band glows with sparkling gold energy when activated. He has sharp eyes, an angular face, close-cropped silver hair, and a stern, judgmental posture that softens into reluctant respect. Keep the purple armor and golden forearm band visible whenever he appears.

## Needle Men

The Needle Men are invading alien robots built from modular metal needles. Each body is a humanoid cluster of interlocking silver needle rods, jointed needle spines, blade-like fingers, and porcupine-like projectile quills. They should look mass-produced but constantly reconfiguring, with red sensor slits and cold chrome reflections. When cut apart, they scatter into broken needle modules and sparks.

## Storyverse City

The battle takes place in a dense futuristic city that may be Jet's responsibility to protect. Use shattered streets, glass towers, elevated transit rails, emergency lights, fleeing civilians, and smoke columns. The city should feel bright and readable despite the chaos, with high-saturation anime colors, teal city lights, red alarms, sparkling purple Lucid Light, rainbow sword glow, and golden power-band blasts.

## Lucid Light and Props

Lucid Light is reality-warping energy wielded by Lucid Lords. It appears as sparkling purple energy that shines like a night sky full of stars. Jet's Lucid Light shears are oversized magical cutting tools made from that purple starlit energy. Jet's rainbow crystal sword is colorful, futuristic, and used here as a healing source, surrounding him with restorative rainbow light. Purple Suit's golden power band fires focused sparkling gold blasts.
