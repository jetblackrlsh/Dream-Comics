# Page Script

All readable story text below must be generated directly inside the page art in caption boxes. Do not add captions, titles, labels, or dialogue during PDF assembly.

## Page 1 Cover

Title text generated inside the art: "05/18/2026: Anywhere City Mall Paradox"

Cover composition: A bright high-saturation anime comic cover. Jet stands in the center of a vast impossible mall atrium with dark-brown skin, full dark-brown beard, long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from a black hoodie, "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and black cross-body belt bag. He holds a glossy black unlimited-funds card in one hand, while his rainbow crystal sword and golden glitter-light energy tether hang at his sides. Endless shelves, balconies, escalators, vehicles, instruments, electronics, clothes, tools, books, toys, furniture, and strange Storyverse artifacts spiral upward behind him. Purple Lucid Light forms an infinity loop between the card, a mall deed, city shoppers, stocked shelves, and ghostly empty shelves. The mood is wonder mixed with intellectual danger.

## Page 2

Layout: three stacked panels, vertical reading order.

Caption boxes:
- "Sleep paralysis opened the door."
- "Lucidity followed me into the Storyverse."
- "Then I arrived alone in Anywhere City."

Visual beats: Top panel shows Jet lying stiff in bed under a black hoodie, dark-brown dreadlocks, full beard, and warm brown eyes half-open while sleep-paralysis shadows press around him and purple Lucid Light sparks near his chest. Middle panel shows Jet floating through the cosmic Storyverse void, surrounded by bubble universes and purple starry Lucid Light, with his rainbow crystal sword and golden energy tether visible. Bottom panel shows Jet stepping onto the surreal street of Anywhere City, where one bank, one police station, one hospital, one apartment building, and many other unique buildings crowd a bright impossible skyline.

## Page 3

Layout: one grand mall reveal panel above three smaller browsing panels.

Caption boxes:
- "Anywhere City had one mall."
- "Inside it was everything currently for sale."
- "Every option waited at once."
- "The black card could buy any of it."

Visual beats: Grand panel shows the entrance of Anywhere City Mall as a luminous glass megastructure with no readable brand names, doors opening into impossible depth. Smaller panels show Jet walking through departments full of clothes, electronics, books, tools, art supplies, musical instruments, food, furniture, vehicles, collectibles, and glowing Storyverse artifacts. Jet studies a sleek black card in his hand, excited but already overwhelmed.

## Page 4

Layout: four panels that grow more crowded and surreal.

Caption boxes:
- "Choice became a maze."
- "If I could buy anything..."
- "...how could I choose one thing?"
- "The easiest answer was everything."

Visual beats: Panel one shows Jet surrounded by branching mall signs and corridors, each direction packed with different goods, with no readable store names. Panel two shows close-ups of Jet's warm brown eyes reflecting countless items and price tags. Panel three shows the black card shining with purple Lucid Light as endless shelves curve around him like a labyrinth. Panel four shows Jet imagining himself at a gigantic checkout counter buying the entire mall, with a glowing deed, stacks of receipts, shopping carts, and every department folding into one enormous purchase.

## Page 5

Layout: one wide ethical-reversal panel above three human-scale panels.

Caption boxes:
- "Then the city entered the equation."
- "This was the only mall."
- "If I bought every item..."
- "...no one else could buy anything."

Visual beats: Wide panel shows Jet looking from the checkout fantasy to the mall concourse filled with city shoppers. The three lower panels show a family choosing groceries, a worker buying tools, and an artist selecting supplies, each depending on the mall. Jet lowers the black card, concerned. The dream city is visible beyond the glass atrium, reinforcing that the mall serves everyone.

## Page 6

Layout: five panels with recursive, magical logic.

Caption boxes:
- "The paradox got stranger."
- "The mall contained every item for sale."
- "If I bought the mall..."
- "...and stopped the sales..."
- "...would the items vanish?"

Visual beats: Panel one shows the whole mall outlined in purple Lucid Light as if it is powered by the concept of sale itself. Panel two shows Jet holding the black card near a glowing mall deed while shelves remain full. Panel three shows a large symbolic switch from "for sale" to a blank unreadable dark state, with no specific readable text besides caption boxes. Panel four shows products dissolving into purple starlight because they are no longer for sale. Panel five shows Jet standing in a vast empty mall, shocked, as the black card reflects his face and an infinity loop of loss.

## Page 7

Layout: one large paradox diagram splash with inset panels.

Caption boxes:
- "I saw the loop."
- "Buy every option."
- "End every sale."
- "Erase the inventory."
- "Own the absence."

Visual beats: Large splash panel shows a cosmic diagram made from mall architecture: the black card leads to the mall deed, the mall deed leads to locked registers, locked registers lead to empty shelves, empty shelves lead back to Jet holding nothing. Inset panels show stocked shelves becoming transparent, shoppers fading into concern, Jet's rainbow sword and golden tether catching purple Lucid Light, and the Storyverse bubble around the mall flickering like a thought experiment becoming reality. The paradox should feel fascinating, not frightening.

## Page 8

Layout: two-panel resolution: quiet checkout above, restored mall below.

Caption boxes:
- "So I bought nothing."
- "The mall stayed possible."
- "The city kept its choices."
- "The discovery was the purchase."

Visual beats: Top panel shows Jet at the checkout counter with the black card unused on the surface, his hand hovering above it but not taking action. Purple Lucid Light forms a calm loop over the card rather than a destructive one. Bottom panel shows Jet walking away through the still-stocked Anywhere City Mall, shoppers browsing peacefully, shelves full, escalators glowing, and every kind of item still available. Jet smiles with quiet fascination, carrying his rainbow sword and golden energy tether, having gained insight instead of possessions.
