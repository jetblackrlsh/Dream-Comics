# Treatment

## Title

05/11/2026: Living Comic Conduit

## Logline

After sleep paralysis drops Jet into a Storyverse world where he manages superpowered celebrities, his usual Lucid Light control is sealed away until a living comic book becomes the only safe conduit home.

## Central Conflict

Jet has authority over the futures of powerful celebrities, but no reliable access to the lucid abilities that normally protect him. When angel-driven buses offer dangerous routes for beings of immense power, Jet must choose restraint over status and learn to route his awareness through a strange living comic book.

## Theme

Lucid control can change shape; sometimes power works through a medium instead of the hand that expects to wield it.

## Ending

Jet refuses the angel buses, opens the living comic, and lets purple Lucid Light flow through its pages. The book folds the celebrity world into panels and returns Jet home, leaving him with a name for the experience: conduit lucidity.

## Emotional Arc

Jet moves from professional confidence, to pressure, to unease, to humility, to wonder as he realizes that the dream has divided his control between his body and the living comic book.

## Page Count

Eight pages: cover plus seven story pages.
