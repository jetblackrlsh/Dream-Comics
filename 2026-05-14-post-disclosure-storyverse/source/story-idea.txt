Dream journal date: 05/14/2026

Jet induced lucidity through sleep paralysis and chose to spawn alone in a Storyverse world reshaped by UAP and alien disclosure. The world tests Jet's fear that politicians would use the unknown to collapse the line between church and state, arguing that aliens and UAP are demons or angels, then insisting that law must reflect their religion.

The world is not as radically dystopian as Jet expected. Daily life continues in familiar ways, but public buildings carry more crosses, more people dress like priests and nuns, and the culture feels newly pressured by official religious certainty. Jet dislikes the dogma, especially the political use of mystery to control others, but recognizes that similar tendencies already existed before disclosure.

The more surprising change is technological. Disclosure caused indie hacking culture to expand. Independent builders now try to recreate alien technology, similar to how inventors in Jet's waking world try to recreate superhero technology. Jet leaves the world with a conflicted hope: technological progress should expand through disclosure without sacrificing religious freedom or cultural identity.
