# Character Bible

## Jet

Man with dark-brown skin, full dark-brown beard, warm brown eyes, long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a black hoodie over a "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. Signature props: a colorful futuristic crystal rainbow sword sheathed or glowing at his side, and a rope-like energy tether made of golden glittery light. His body language moves from startled and braced to calm, floating, and reverent.

## Peaceful Aliens

Only brief, distant figures. They should look mysterious, gentle, and nonthreatening: slender luminous silhouettes and small biomechanical forms drifting in orbit. They do not wear human military gear, do not attack, and do not dominate the page.

## Earth

The most important recurring visual. Hyperreal blue oceans, white cloud systems, visible continents, bright sunlit half, shadowed night half, thin glowing atmosphere, massive scale. During the climax it fractures into luminous plates, then reforms as if time rewinds.
