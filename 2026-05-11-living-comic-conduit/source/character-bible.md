# Character Bible

## Jet

Adult man with dark-brown skin, full dark-brown beard, warm brown eyes, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a black hoodie over a "Dream Anime" graphic tee, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. His signature items are a colorful futuristic rainbow crystal sword and a rope-like golden glittering energy tether. His normal lucid power is Lucid Light: sparkling purple reality-warping energy that shines like a night sky full of stars. In this issue, the Lucid Light is dim around Jet's hands and stronger around the living comic book.

## Living Comic Book

A large strange comic book with a glossy black cover, silver corner caps, glowing purple page edges, and a central eye-shaped constellation symbol made of Lucid Light. It looks ancient and futuristic at the same time. The book floats, opens itself, and drinks in Jet's purple Lucid Light. Its pages show moving panels that mirror the world around Jet. It should feel alive without having a face, mouth, arms, or speech bubbles.

## Angel Bus Drivers

Tall serene angels with luminous skin, feathered wings, white-and-gold driver uniforms, calm unreadable eyes, and halos shaped like transit rings. They drive colossal celestial buses built for beings of incredible power. They are not villains; they are beautiful, dangerous gatekeepers.

## Superpowered Celebrities

A rotating group of glamorous powered performers in a cosmic talent agency: a star-haired singer radiating solar fire, a crystalline actor with diamond skin, a masked speed idol trailing blue lightning, and a floating gravity wrestler surrounded by orbiting spotlights. They should look famous, powerful, and anxious under Jet's decisions.

## Setting

A Storyverse celebrity-management world: luxury agency offices, neon award stages, cosmic billboards, velvet ropes, cameras, and windows showing bubble universes floating in a void. The bus depot is a vast celestial terminal with hovering routes and angel-driven buses large enough for gods. The final homecoming shows Leon, Jet's ghostly UFO, as an ethereal green glowing flying saucer waiting in the cosmic void near Storyverse bubble universes.
