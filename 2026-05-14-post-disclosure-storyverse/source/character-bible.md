# Character Bible

## Jet

Jet is a lucid dream traveler with dark-brown skin, warm brown eyes, a full dark-brown beard, and long dark-brown dreadlocks spilling from beneath a black hoodie. He wears a "Dream Anime" graphic tee under the hoodie, black athletic pants with a white stripe, white sneakers, and a black cross-body belt bag. He carries a rainbow sword that resembles a colorful futuristic crystal sword and an energy tether that resembles a rope made of golden glittery light. Keep his outfit, dreadlocks, beard, and belt bag consistent on every page. His expression range moves from guarded suspicion to wary curiosity to thoughtful hope.

## The Disclosure Politician

A composed public official who uses UAP disclosure to argue that aliens are demons or angels and that law should reflect religion. The politician wears a dark formal suit with a white clerical-style collar accent, a lapel cross pin, and polished campaign-stage posture. They should read as institutional power rather than a cartoon villain.

## The Civic Crowd

Ordinary city residents in a familiar modern world subtly changed by disclosure politics. Some wear everyday clothes, some wear priest-like black coats, and some wear nun-like veils. The crowd should feel mixed and realistic, not a mob: curious, uncertain, devout, skeptical, tired, and absorbed in daily life.

## The Indie Alien-Tech Builder

A young independent inventor in a cluttered garage workshop. They wear practical work clothes, safety goggles, fingerless gloves, and a patched jacket covered with tiny circuit diagrams. Their signature prop is a homemade levitating alien-tech engine: silver rings, teal lights, antigravity sparks, and improvised cables.

## Storyverse Setting Motifs

The Storyverse is a multiverse of cosmic bubble universes floating in a vast void. This specific world looks like a near-future city after UAP disclosure: familiar streets and civic buildings, more crosses on public architecture, more clergy-like clothing in public, news screens discussing UAP as angels or demons, and a countercurrent of garages, maker spaces, alien schematics, levitating parts, and teal-blue antigravity light. Jet's Lucid Light appears as sparkling purple energy like a starry night sky when he enters and leaves.
