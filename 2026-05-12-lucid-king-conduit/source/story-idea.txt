Dream journal entry dated 05/12/2026.

Jet induced lucidity via sleep paralysis and arrived alone beneath a starlit sky beside an oddly normal toucan. The toucan flew into his arms, confusing him because it did not talk, show powers, or carry any magic item.

As the dream shifted away from the beach and past palm trees, a wonder-filled city appeared. Superpowered beings flew through the air in colorful costumes. Jet's Lucid Light was not in his own possession. Instead, it had taken the form of a separate superhuman: a short black buzzcut, a regal purple cheetah-print cape, and a gold-and-red futuristic leather bodysuit. This Lucid Light avatar ruled the city as a tyrant king.

Other superhumans wanted to overthrow him, but Jet did not see him as a threat. Jet warned them not to attack. Using the connection he still had to the Lucid Light inside the king's core, Jet changed the ruler's heart. The tyrant became a king of the people.

This became the second dream in a row where Jet's lucidity manifested separately from himself. Instead of having direct power to do anything he wished, Jet could use Lucid Light infused beings or items as conduits to make his will become reality. The tension is that true lucidity may now require searching for those special conduits.
