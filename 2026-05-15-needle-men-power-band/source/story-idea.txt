Dream journal date: 05/15/2026

Jet induced lucidity through sleep paralysis and arrived alone on a Storyverse world. A stranger in a purple battle suit recognized him immediately and wore a golden power band on his arm. The stranger demanded to know what Jet had done with his own band. Jet answered instinctually that he did not need it.

The answer angered the stranger. He motioned toward a city under attack by invading alien robots called Needle Men, made from modular metal needles and firing needle projectiles like porcupine spines. When Jet asked why the stranger had not already saved the city with his own golden band, the stranger scoffed that protecting Jet's world was not his responsibility.

Jet leaped into battle with confidence, saying he would show the stranger how he gets things done. He summoned shears made of sparkling purple Lucid Light and sliced apart the Needle Men. As the robots fired piercing needle volleys into him, Jet used his rainbow crystal sword as a magical source of healing energy instead of as a weapon, rejuvenating himself and staying in the fight.

Inspired by Jet's action, the stranger in the purple battle suit joined the battle. Using the golden power band, he fired sparkling gold energy blasts that blew apart Needle Men in single strikes. Jet woke before the battle could end, fascinated by the idea that this world was supposedly his responsibility to defend and wondering how the stranger knew him.
